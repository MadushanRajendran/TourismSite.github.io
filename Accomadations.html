Taj Samudra offers enchanting views of the mesmerising Indian Ocean, Galle Face Green and the vibrant city of Colombo. Visit them on Tajhotels

The exotic hotel boasts 300 rooms with 11 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens.It features an outdoor pool, 7 dining options and tennis courts. Bright and airy, elegant rooms have classic wood furnishings and comfortable seating areas. They are fitted with cable TV, a fridge and a tea/coffee maker. For leisure, relaxing massages, a well-equipped fitness center and squash courts are available. Attentive staff can provide business and childcare services. Guests can enjoy traditional dishes at Golden Dragon Chinese Restaurant and Navratna Indian Restaurant. Other dining highlights include the Steak and Grill House. Taj Samudra is a 45-minute drive from the Bandaranaike Airport and provides free parking. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.

A personal tropical sanctuary that is perfect for escaping the city, Shangri-La Hotel, with direct access to the most extensive international shopping mall in Sri Lanka, Shangri-La’s own One Galle Face Mall. Visit them on Shangri-La.

The hotel offers the finest accommodation in Colombo, an exciting new dining and social scene and the largest and extensive hotel conference and event facilities with 541 rooms and suites.Set to be the city’s dining destination, Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo’s restaurants and bars include Capital Bar & Grill, presenting tasty steaks and seafood grills with a wide selection of whiskey. Kaema Sutra is a new-age Sri Lankan bar and restaurant. Sapphyr Lounge is a place to meet with friends and family over simple indulgences. There is a Pool Bar overlooking the Indian Ocean and it is perfect for light refreshments. Chi, The Spa is a place of personal peace and enchantment offering holistic therapies for the body, mind and soul. The Health Club features state-of-the-art fitness equipment and personal trainers to ensure you stay true to your fitness regime when away from home. Daily buffet breakfast and dinner are served at either Capital Bar & Grill or Kaema Sutra.

Bejeweled as one of the finest five-star Colombo hotels,The Kingsbury located in-between Galle Face Green, the World Trade Centre and the Dutch Hospital Precinct.Visit them on The Kingsbury.

Elegantly-designed yet tasteful and spacious, the rooms have oversize windows offering breathtaking views of the Colombo Harbor and the Galle Face Green. Bright and airy, they are fitted with cable TV, a mini-bar and tea and coffee making facilities. Each room has an en-suite marble bathroom. Guests can leisurely soak in the outdoor swimming pool, wind down at the Senses Spa or work out at the well-equipped gym. The 24-hour butler service ensures immaculate relaxation at all times.he restaurants at The Kingsbury Colombo offer extensive menus with various cuisines, that cater to a variety of dietary needs. The Kingsbury’s opulent lobby bar, The Kings,offers heavenly pub grub and tantalizing cocktails, with live music. Yue Chuan specializes in authentic Cantonese & Szechuan cuisine, as well as Sri Lankan Chinese fusion dishes.

Cinnamon Grand is luxurious 5 Star City Hotel located in the center of Colombo. Cinnamon Grand offers Rooms and Suites, The Spa, 2 Outdoor Swimming Pools, Fitness Center.Visit them on The Cinnamon Grand.

They have 14 Dining Options Including “Lagoon” the best seafood restaurant in the city and free Wi-Fi. 22 mi’s from the airport with Audi Sedan or SUV Airport Pickup/Drop options.Cinnamon Grand offers a range of services for each traveler and room from Pillow menus, Turn-Down service, Breakfast in Bed Options, In-Room Safes, Tea & Coffee Facilities. The Galadari Hotel is a finest star class hotel in Sri Lanka with the best of dinning, accommodation and entertainment facilities. This 450 roomed beauty is located facing the Indian Ocean.Visit them on The Galadari.

Step-in to be lost in unearthly cuisines, cosy hideouts, heavenly surrounding and the best of services, which other hotels in Sri Lanka could not offer. Galadari Hotel Sri Lanka; one of the finest hotels in Sri Lanka is not only the best place to relax, eat and indulge, but is also the finest place to celebrate. The air conditioned guest rooms at Hotel Galadari have large windows that offer plenty of natural light. Rooms are equipped with a cable TV, tea/coffee making facilities and a personal safe. The Coffee Shop serves a daily buffet breakfast, while the rooftop California Grill offers fresh seafood and steaks. Other dining options include Chinese cuisine at Stix, Arabian food at Sheherezade Restaurant and pastries at Cafe 64. Drinks are available at the hotel's 2 bars and karaoke lounge. For a refreshing workout, guests can visit the well-equipped fitness center or enjoy a game of tennis. The Galadari also provides a tour desk and car rentals.
